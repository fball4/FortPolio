Web Development

This was part two of a game that I made in first year, first semester. You play as a brave leader, Weebo (yes, the floating head), that is trying to bring change to a world turned communist. The communist leaders are trying to kill you by throwing hammers and sickles at you, so you have to use the arrow keys to move around to dodge them, and you can click on the items thrown to get rid of them. There are sound tweens that are attached to the item clicks. Two of them are pinch harmonics, and one of them is me doing a death metal scream haha.
